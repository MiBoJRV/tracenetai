Applications Across the Financial Ecosystem

The platform's modular architecture allows it to operate across multiple layers of the financial landscape —from individual account monitoring to institutional compliance infrastructure.
Built on Gemini's AI framework, it interprets transaction data, evaluates behavioral risk, and acts through verified financial APIs.

Different users. One system of transparency.

Personal Financial Awareness and Protection

For individual users, the system functions as a continuous monitoring environment that provides real-time visibility into every transaction. It identifies recurring charges, hidden subscriptions, and potential unauthorized activity — and, when required, initiates authorized refund or dispute requests through the user’s financial institution.

Use cases:

• Detect recurring or duplicate payments
• Identify unauthorized transactions before they accumulate
• Receive structured reports with fraud scores and compliance context
• Automate chargeback or recall processes securely

Individuals gain clarity over their digital finances and reduce losses from unnoticed activity or subscription leakage.

Corporate Transaction Oversight

For enterprises, the platform acts as a financial integrity layer — monitoring all outgoing and incoming transactions to ensure they align with internal policies, vendor agreements, and regional compliance standards.

Use cases:

• Detect unapproved or duplicate vendor payments
• Analyze financial exposure across accounts and subsidiaries
• Generate automated compliance and audit reports
• Identify early indicators of account misuse or insider fraud

Financial institutions gain a scalable, regulator-aligned AI layer for fraud management and customer protection.

AI-Powered Fraud and Compliance Infrastructure

Banks, fintech companies, and exchanges integrate the system through API to enhance their fraud detection, transaction traceability, and regulatory response capabilities. The Gemini-based core delivers contextual risk assessment for both fiat and crypto operations, improving precision and reducing false positives.

Use cases:

• Real-time anomaly detection across payment networks
• Automated escalation of high-risk transaction chains
• Instant generation of incident reports for compliance teams
• Support for chargeback and dispute automation through regulated APIs

Financial institutions gain a scalable, regulator-aligned AI layer for fraud management and customer protection.

Transparent Oversight and Traceability

For supervisory bodies and regulatory auditors, the platform provides a neutral AI-driven view of transaction networks —with explainable reasoning, immutable audit trails, and standardized data export formats.

Use cases:

• Cross-platform tracing of suspicious fund movement
• Automated AML/KYC anomaly detection
• Centralized visualization of institution-wide financial flows
• On-demand audit data with full explainability metadata

Regulators receive structured, verified, and explainable financial intelligence aligned with global compliance frameworks.

Unified Ecosystem Connectivity

Through open APIs, the system connects to financial, blockchain, and compliance infrastructures — enabling data exchange between individual, corporate, and institutional layers without compromising privacy or regulatory boundaries.

Integrations include:

• Retail and commercial banks
• Payment gateways and acquirers
• Major crypto exchanges and custodians
• Compliance management systems
• Governmental AML & financial intelligence units

Impact Across All Stakeholders

By introducing explainable AI into the financial ecosystem, the platform improves visibility, reduces systemic fraud, and enhances compliance efficiency across all participants.

Shared benefits:

• Unified fraud-risk framework across fiat and crypto ecosystems
• Automated compliance actions with regulator-approved logic
• Reduced operational costs for investigation and reporting
• Enhanced user trust through transparency and accountability
