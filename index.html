AI Infrastructure for Global Transaction Transparency

Powered by Google Gemini technology and integrated with banks and crypto platforms worldwide, our system provides real-time visibility into fund movements, risk scoring, and automated compliance actions.

Explore the Technology

Understanding the Platform

The system combines large-scale financial data modeling, behavioral analytics,and regulatory intelligence to interpret the flow of money across connected accounts and digital assets.

It operates as a neutral analytical layer — securely connected to financial institutions through verified APIs, providing structured insight into every transaction chain and its associated risk.

Connectivity

Direct API integrations with regulated banks and major crypto exchanges.

Intelligence

Gemini-based models optimized for real-time financial reasoning.

Integrity

Fully compliant with GDPR, AMLD6, and open-banking frameworks.

Core Functional Layers

Analysis

Processes multi-channel financial data and identifies irregular activity using Gemini's contextual understanding.

Risk Evaluation

Applies AI-driven scoring and behavioral clustering to determine exposure to fraud, unauthorized use, or compliance breach.

Automated Response

When risk thresholds are reached, the system can initiate authorized procedures — including chargeback, recall, or dispute actions — directly through institutional APIs.

Access and Regulatory Framework

Because the platform interacts with live financial networks, access is granted only after completion of a short compliance onboarding required by global regulators.

This ensures lawful data exchange, verified identity, and full accountability of every operation executed through the AI infrastructure.

Compliance is not an add-on — it's an integral part of the system design.

Designed for Transparency, Built for Trust

The platform helps institutions and individuals detect irregular financial behavior, reduce exposure to fraud, and maintain full visibility over both fiat and digital transactions.

It functions as a permanent monitoring layer that ensures each movement of value is traceable, explainable, and compliant.
